# Templates

These are the Health Shield customer success story templates. Each one is ready to fill in, with friendly prompts to guide you. Copy the block you need and replace the bits in \[square brackets].

A few quick reminders as you write:

- Keep it warm, clear, and simple – read it aloud to check it sounds natural
- Use British English spelling throughout
- Keep paragraphs short – three sentences is plenty
- Tell the customer's story in their own words – tell, don't sell

---

## Standard case study (full format)

```
# [Customer name]: [Result or transformation statement]

## About the customer

[1 to 2 warm sentences introducing the customer, e.g. "The Southern GP Federation Support Unit supports 71 GP practices across Northern Ireland. They want to make sure their team feels cared for and supported every day."]

| | |
|---|---|
| **Industry** | [Industry] |
| **Size** | [Number of staff] |
| **Work environment** | [E.g. healthcare settings, front-line roles, office teams] |

---

## The challenge

[2 to 3 short paragraphs on what life was like before Health Shield stepped in. Keep sentences short and easy to read.]

- What was getting in the way of their team's health and happiness? (e.g. high absences, everyday stress, the cost of everyday healthcare)
- How did this affect the business and the people working there?
- What had they tried before to help their team?

> "[Quote from the customer describing the challenge, e.g. 'Our staff were under a lot of stress, and we needed to find a way to help everyone feel supported.']"
> – [Name, Title, Company]

---

## The solution

[2 to 3 short paragraphs on how you worked together to build a health cash plan that fits their people. Focus on how we made things simple and supportive.]

- What support did the plan offer? (e.g. 24/7 GP access, mental health support, money back on dental, optical, and physio)
- How did we shape the plan to suit their people?
- How easy was it to get started?

**How we helped**

- **A plan built for everyone** – making sure all staff get the help they need
- **Simple digital access** – everyday healthcare support through our easy-to-use app
- **Money back on everyday costs** – dental, optical, physio, and more
- **24/7 GP access and mental health support** – help that's always close to hand
- **A dedicated account manager** – friendly support along the way

---

## The difference it made

[2 to 3 short paragraphs on the outcomes. Blend real stories from staff with any numbers you have, to show the human impact.]

**The real impact**

- **Staff stories:** [E.g. "One team member told us how getting money back on their physio helped them recover quickly and get back to doing what they love."]
- **A happier workplace:** [E.g. "The team now feels much more valued and cared for by their employer."]
- **Favourite benefits:** [E.g. "The 24/7 GP access has been a real favourite, with lots of people saying how much time it saves them."]
- **Leadership view:** [E.g. "The HR team told us they've seen a real boost in morale and a drop in stress across the business."]

**The numbers (if you have them)**

| Metric | Before | After | The change |
|---|---|---|---|
| Absences | [Value] | [Value] | [% drop] |
| Engagement | [Value] | [Value] | [% rise] |
| Claims made | [Value] | [Value] | [Number] |

> "[Quote from the customer about the difference it made, e.g. 'Working with Health Shield has changed everything for us. Our people finally feel supported and valued.']"
> – [Name, Title, Company]

---

## What's next

[1 short paragraph on how the customer plans to keep supporting their team's wellbeing, and how we'll carry on caring for them too.]

---

*Ready to build a healthier, happier workplace? [Get in touch / Find out more]*
```
