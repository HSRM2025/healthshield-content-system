# Examples

These are worked examples to guide you when writing Health Shield customer success stories. Each one shows the raw inputs, then the finished output. Use them as a steer, not a strict template – every customer's story is different, and warmth always comes first.

A quick reminder before you start: we tell the customer's story in their own words. We celebrate how they care for their people, with a little help from us. Tell, don't sell.

---

## A note on using these examples

These examples show the range of stories you can tell – from numbers-led results to heart-led culture pieces, and everything in between.

Whichever shape your story takes, keep these in mind:

- **Lead with the customer and their people**, not our products – tell, don't sell
- **Use the customer's own words** wherever you can, so it sounds like them
- **Stay clear, fair, and not misleading** with any claims or numbers
- **Only publish once both approval stages are signed off** – the words first (Stage 1), then the finished assets and where they'll appear (Stage 2)
- **Use British English** and keep the tone warm, friendly, and caring throughout
